Who We Are

Welcome to ARG Instrumentación y Metrología, your go-to partner for enterprise consulting services for specialized equipment.

With over 20 years of experience in the market, we have established ourselves as a trusted provider of calibration and maintenance solutions.

Our team of experts is based in Heredia, Costa Rica, but we are always ready to travel to your site to evaluate the design of your machine and provide the solutions you need.
